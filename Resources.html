Resourcces

1. https://www.upmc.com/services/south-central-pa/women/services/pregnancy-childbirth/new-moms/postpartum-depression this site offers a HOPE group but they only meet in Carlise.

2. https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/groups/pennsylvania?category=pregnancy-prenatal-postpartum This link brings you to only a few support groups (overeating, anger management, none specifically for postpartum depression)

3. https://www.cdc.gov/reproductivehealth/depression/resources.html This is a great website for additional information and some important links to NAMI, NSPL, and PSI. These are national sites without much information or help for local residents.

4. https://moveforwardpa.com/services/counseling-for-mothers/ (Lancaster, Hershey, Pittsburgh, Lehigh Valley, online)

5. https://cap4kids.org/york/parent-handouts/pregnancy-resources-and-infants/

6. https://mchb.hrsa.gov/national-maternal-mental-health-hotline This is a good site but a government site on maternal mental health but requires a call.

7. https://www.aap.org/ Good site for pediatrics and postpartum depression links to "healthy children website".

8. https://healthychildren.org/English/ages-stages/prenatal/delivery-beyond/Pages/dads-can-get-postpartum-depression-too.aspx
